05 · WHAT LOVECLAW WON'T DO ♥ PROMISE
THIS PROTOCOL IS OPINIONATED. IT EXISTS TO MAKE COMMITMENTS LEGIBLE — NOT TO SURVEIL, COERCE, OR PUNISH. THESE ARE NON-NEGOTIABLE.
001 · CONSENT

NO RULE WITHOUT BOTH SIGNATURES.

EVERY RULE IS CO-AUTHORED AND CO-SIGNED. EITHER PARTNER CAN VETO. UNILATERAL MONITORING IS NEVER A FEATURE.

002 · LOCALITY

RAW SIGNALS NEVER LEAVE THE DEVICE.

ONLY ATTESTATIONS CROSS THE WIRE. LOCATION, MESSAGES, AND APP CONTENTS STAY ON-DEVICE ALWAYS.

003 · EXIT

LEAVING IS A FIRST-CLASS ACTION.

ENDING THE PROTOCOL RETURNS ALL COLLATERAL WITH NO PENALTY. LEAVING A RELATIONSHIP IS NEVER A BREACH.
